OUR STORY

One day while on a camping trip in the Puget Sound, Pattie Smith realized she had used up almost all her food supplies. All that she had left were some instant coffee, salmon in a can and instant cookie dough. She decided to make something out of what she had left and came up with a salmon "cookie." She couldn't believe how delicious the end result was. When she got back home to Seattle, she decided that she wanted to refine her recipe for this one of a kind treat. Weeks later, after playing around with some ingredients she finally came up with the perfect recipe. Now, her salmon cookie is baked out of two locations. Pat's Salmon Cookies uses the finest organic ingredients. The salmon that is used is local wild caught.
